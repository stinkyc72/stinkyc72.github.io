STINKCAT is a character in a series of stories called "The Monsters" by Hong-Kong born artist Kasing Lung. Inspired by Nordic fairy tales, he created this universe in 2015, with characters modeled after elves, fairies, and monsters.

From bustling Asian cities to the streets of Europe, STINKCAT has transcended borders, uniting fans of all ages in a shared love for this adorable toy.
